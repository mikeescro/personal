About me:

I'm a web developer from Mount Laurel, NJ. Originally born and raised in Burlington, NJ. I have a soft spot for anything tech or science related. I love playing video games, building/upgrading my custom PC, playing guitar, making music, and watching a good documentary. I was introduced to coding as a teen back in the Myspace days with some light css/html work and ever since then, I've been hooked. I started getting myself more acclimated back in 2017 with the Full Stack Web Developer - TechDegree program over at Team Treehouse. Unfortunately, life derailed my plans and I had to put it on the back burner. Fast forward to 2023 and I am back at it with a Full Stack Web Developer course from a website called Zero to Mastery. I'm striving to be the best programmer I can be. I want to be part of the new wave in tech. I want to help create the next big thing.. with you. So, check out some of my work and feel free to drop me a line!
